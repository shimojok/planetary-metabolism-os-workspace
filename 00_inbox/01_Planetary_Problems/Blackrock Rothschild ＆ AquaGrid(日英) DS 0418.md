  微生物を用いた土壌修復技術で、森林火災後の生態系回復を加速。

  海水淡水化と津波防護機能を備えた沿岸インフラ。

---

### **1. Core Components**  
#### **(1) Technological Foundation**  
- **AquaGrid**:  
  Smart water management system integrating underground water storage, renewable energy generation, and disaster resilience.  
  - Floodwater storage during storms; agricultural water supply during droughts.  
  - Micro-hydropower for regional energy self-sufficiency.  
- **MBT Sustainable Cycle**:  
  Microbial soil restoration technology to accelerate post-wildfire ecosystem recovery.  
- **AquaTower**:  
  Coastal infrastructure combining desalination and tsunami protection.  

#### **(2) Funding Mechanism**  
- **Climate Bonds**:  
  BlackRock-led issuance of "Green Infrastructure Bonds" for GRIF projects, restricted to SBTi-certified initiatives.  
- **International Climate Fund (ICF)**:  
  Pooled contributions from advanced nations (0.5% of GDP) and reinsurance risk pools to provide low-interest loans/guarantees.  
- **Insurance Incentives**:  
  Munich Re and others offer up to 30% premium discounts for regions adopting GRIF infrastructure.  

#### **(3) Governance Structure**  
- **GRIF Council**:  
  Coalition of BlackRock (investment strategy), Rothschild Foundation (social equity), UNDP, and G20 nations.  
  - Prioritizes projects using AI-driven "Climate Vulnerability Maps."  
- **Regional Implementation Units**:  
  Local governments, NGOs, and tech firms (e.g., AquaGrid developers) collaborate on execution.

  Real-time tracking of fund usage and CO2 reduction.
