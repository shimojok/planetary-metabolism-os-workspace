Proposal for Regenerative Agriculture and Sustainable Food Systems  
Based on MBT Technology and the AGRIX Platform

Prepared for:  
Rockefeller Foundation & Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

---

**1. Executive Summary**

The 2021 United Nations Food Systems Summit aimed to transform global food systems through technology-driven approaches, emphasizing precision agriculture, genetic engineering, and digitalization. However, it faced criticism for marginalizing smallholder farmers and over-relying on industrial solutions. This proposal offers an alternative regenerative model based on ecological principles and biotechnology, addressing unresolved global food issues such as hunger, soil degradation, climate change, and supply chain instability.

Our approach integrates MBT55 microbial technology, AGRIX Platform, ANE (Agro Nutritional Education), and ACAIN (Agro Carbon and Investment Network) to create sustainable, inclusive, and circular food systems.

---

**2. Identified Problems from the 2021 UN Food Summit**

- Hunger and malnutrition remain widespread.
    
- Soil fertility is declining due to overuse of chemical inputs.
    
- GHG emissions from agriculture are increasing.
    
- Droughts, floods, and disasters disrupt agricultural productivity.
    
- Agricultural inputs and food prices remain volatile.
    
- Food loss and waste are rampant across the value chain.
    
- Technological solutions often exclude smallholder farmers.
    

---

**3. Our Regenerative Solution**

**MBT Sustainable Cycle and AGRIX Platform**

- **Soil Regeneration**: MBT55 enhances physical and microbial soil properties, enabling better nutrient uptake and plant health.
    
- **Ecological Hypercycle**: Recycles organic waste into high-performance microbial fertilizers, restoring biogeochemical balance.
    
- **Chemical Input Reduction**: Enhances NPK utilization efficiency, breaks down pesticide residues, reducing dependency on chemicals.
    
- **Yield and Quality Improvement**: Increases edible parts of crops and post-harvest freshness, contributing to food loss reduction.
    

**Platform Functions**:

- **AGRIX**: Manages material flow, traceability, and fair trade.
    
- **ANE**: Provides agricultural education and local knowledge transfer.
    
- **ACAIN**: Enables investment, carbon credit accounting, and policy linkage.
