To Dr. Rajiv J. Shah, The Rockefeller Foundation
We are deeply impressed by the activities of your Foundation, and we have particularly noted the innovative initiatives being promoted under your leadership.
We are advancing projects—the AGRIX Project, MBT Sustainable Cycle, MBT Food & Herbal Probiotics, and HealthBook Project—that hold the potential to unleash people's potential, contribute to society, and simultaneously generate economic benefits. We are confident that these projects resonate with the vision of your Foundation in the following ways:
Realizing People's Dreams and Contributing to Society
 * AGRIX Project contributes to the revitalization of local communities and food security through sustainable agriculture, supporting the dreams of farmers.
 * MBT Sustainable Cycle aims to build an environmentally conscious circular society, creating a better future for coming generations.
 * MBT Food & Herbal Probiotics contributes to improving people's wellness through innovative food and health products, supporting the realization of healthy and fulfilling lives.
 * HealthBook Project provides a platform for personalized health management and information sharing, helping everyone manage their health and gain better access to medical care.
Enjoying Economic Benefits
 * These projects will bring economic benefits to participants and local communities through the creation of new industries, an increase in employment opportunities, and the establishment of sustainable business models.
Fusing Global Knowledge
 * We believe these projects will facilitate the sharing of knowledge and experiences across borders, serving as a platform where researchers, entrepreneurs, and community leaders worldwide can collaborate to create innovative solutions for global challenges.
We sincerely hope to contribute to your Foundation's mission of "promoting the well-being of humanity throughout the world." We would be honored to have the opportunity to discuss these projects in detail, as we believe they can create synergies with the initiatives under Dr. Shah's leadership.
Sincerely,
