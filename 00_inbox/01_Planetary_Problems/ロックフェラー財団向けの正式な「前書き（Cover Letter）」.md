**拝啓**

このたび、MBT55技術とMBT Sustainable Cycleモデルをご紹介する資料をお届けいたします。

- 食料安全保障
- 環境再生
- 廃棄物問題の解決
- 持続可能な地域経済の創出

# 【English Version】Cover Letter

---

**Dear Mr. Ehsani,**
Thank you for your approval.

I am pleased to submit the attached documents introducing the MBT55 technology and the MBT Sustainable Cycle model.  
This project is not merely aligned with the fundamental philosophy of regenerative agriculture—restoring soil and ecosystems—but goes further by practically replicating and accelerating the **Ecological Hypercycle** that underpins the Earth's metabolism.

MBT55, powered by over 120 microbial strains, completes the conventional organic matter decomposition and humification processes—typically requiring years—**within just 24 hours**.  
Meanwhile, the MBT Sustainable Cycle connects agriculture, livestock, food processing, distribution, restaurant sectors, forestry, education, and governmental organizations, establishing an **Organic Waste Network** that reduces disposal costs and advances carbon neutrality.

This system directly contributes to:

- Food security
- Environmental restoration
- Waste management innovation
- Sustainable local economic development

We firmly believe that this model represents a **next-generation implementation of regenerative agriculture**, and it deeply resonates with the Rockefeller Foundation’s vision for a sustainable future for both humanity and the planet.

We sincerely hope that these materials will contribute to your regenerative agriculture initiatives.  
Thank you very much for your time and consideration.

**Sincerely,**
Kaz Shimojo@Tokyo
