#### Food Systems Call to Action - Climate Champions

[Food Systems Call to Action - Climate Champions](https://climatechampions.unfccc.int/system/food-systems-call-to-action/)
(Below is an overview of the linked site)

This site is an introduction page for the campaign “Transforming Food Systems: A Call to Action for People, Nature, and Climate” organized by the Climate Champions. This campaign aims to create a common vision and expand actions and ambitions to achieve food systems that are beneficial for people, nature, and climate by 2030. It also aims to utilize food systems as one of the main solutions for people, nature, and climate.

As can be seen from the related articles, this campaign addresses the following issues:

- Food systems contribute significantly to greenhouse gas emissions that cause climate change, accounting for about 30% of global emissions.
- Food systems also have a negative impact on natural diversity and ecosystem health, causing problems such as land degradation, deforestation, water depletion, and pollution.
- Food systems also provide inadequate services for people’s health and well-being, with about 26% of the world’s population suffering from malnutrition and hunger, and about 40% suffering from diet-related diseases such as obesity and diabetes.

Thus, food systems can be said to have serious problems in the three aspects of people, nature, and climate. This campaign urges the following actions as a priority to solve these problems:

- Formulate and implement concrete goals and plans for transforming food systems.
- Develop and disseminate innovative technologies and practices to enhance the sustainability and resilience of food systems.
- Cooperate and strengthen dialogue and partnership with stakeholders and interested parties of food systems.
- Raise awareness and knowledge about food system transformation and promote participation and responsibility of consumers and citizens.
