### Nigeria Development Plan ✕ AGRIX Project

BioNexus Holdings, as a company providing agri-information and financial services, aims to transform the global food system, restore degraded soils, and address climate change through the implementation of the AGRIX Platform. At the heart of this project is the "MBT Sustainable Cycle," which seeks to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability in Africa.

#### Key Components of the AGRIX Platform:
- **AgriWare**: Provides MBT technology information and cultivation management systems.
- **SafetyChain**: Ensures safety and traceability of agricultural production.
- **AgriChain/World Foods Open Market**: Supports the distribution and trading of agricultural products on the global market.

#### Core Technology - MBT Sustainable Cycle:
- **MBT55 (NB Bacteria) and MBT Fermentation Machines**: Convert food waste and livestock manure into functional compost and feed within 24 hours.

**Advantages**:
- Reduction of waste disposal costs.
- Production of high-quality, domestically produced functional compost.
- Improvement in agricultural productivity in Africa.
- Increase in agricultural income and enhancement of food security.
- Reduction of food loss by over 30% due to improved freshness retention.

#### BioValley Network:
- Establishment of 3,000 BioValley sites across Africa, focusing on Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa.
- Support for seamless agri-business operations from material procurement to production, processing, trading, distribution, and recovery.

With the collaboration between BioNexus and Amazon, the AGRIX Platform will be built on Amazon AWS. Each BioValley will function as Amazon's food fulfillment center, strengthening Amazon's food business and contributing to food security in Africa. Agricultural products will be sold on Amazon's online sales site.
