**サーキュラーエコノミー**
サーキュラーエコノミーとは、資源を無駄にせず、繰り返し使えるようにする経済システムのことです。従来の経済システムは、資源を使い切って廃棄する「線形型」の経済システムでしたが、サーキュラーエコノミーは、資源を循環させ続ける「循環型」の経済システムです。

サーキュラーエコノミーは、以下の3つの原則に基づいています。
* **リデュース（削減）**：資源の使用量を減らす。
* **リユース（再利用）**：資源を繰り返し使う。
* **リサイクル（再生利用）**：資源を再び資源として使う。

**サーキュラーモデルの本質**
サーキュラーモデルとは、サーキュラーエコノミーを実現するための具体的な方法や仕組みのことです。サーキュラーモデルには、以下のようなものがあります。
* **製品設計**：製品を長持ちさせ、再利用やリサイクルを容易にするための設計を行う。
* **ビジネスモデル**：製品の所有権ではなく、使用権を販売するなど、資源の循環を促すビジネスモデルを構築する。
* **技術革新**：リデュース、リユース、リサイクルを容易にする技術を開発する。

**サーキュラーエコノミーの本来の必要性**
サーキュラーエコノミーは、以下の理由から必要とされています。
* **資源の枯渇**：地球上の資源は有限であり、限りなく使い続けることはできません。サーキュラーエコノミーは、資源の枯渇を防ぐために必要です。
* **環境問題**：資源の使い捨てによる環境汚染は、地球温暖化や海洋汚染などの深刻な環境問題を引き起こしています。サーキュラーエコノミーは、環境問題を解決するために必要です。
* **経済成長**：サーキュラーエコノミーは、資源の効率的な利用による経済成長を促します。
サーキュラーエコノミーは、持続可能な社会を実現するために、今後ますます重要になると考えられています。

さて、私達も、食料生産性を続けるにあたり、土壌・肥料・水などの資源とエネルギーの消費を抑えなければなりません。

### Proposal for Constructing a Circular Model
*December 17, 2023 by Kaz Shimojo*

#### Circular Economy

A Circular Economy refers to an economic system that minimizes waste and enables the repeated use of resources. Unlike traditional linear economic systems that deplete resources and discard them, the Circular Economy is based on the continuous circulation of resources.

The Circular Economy is founded on three principles:
- **Reduce**: Decrease the amount of resource usage.
- **Reuse**: Repetitively use resources.
- **Recycle**: Reutilize resources as resources again.

#### Essence of the Circular Model

The Circular Model is the concrete methodology and structure to achieve the Circular Economy. It encompasses the following elements:
- **Product Design**: Design products for durability and ease of reuse and recycling.
- **Business Model**: Establish business models that promote resource circulation, such as selling usage rights rather than ownership.
- **Technological Innovation**: Develop technologies that facilitate reduction, reuse, and recycling.

#### Inherent Necessity of the Circular Economy

The Circular Economy is deemed essential for the following reasons:
- **Resource Depletion**: Earth's resources are finite, and perpetual consumption is unsustainable. The Circular Economy is necessary to prevent resource depletion.
- **Environmental Issues**: Disposable use of resources leads to environmental pollution, causing severe problems like global warming and ocean contamination. The Circular Economy is essential to address environmental issues.
- **Economic Growth**: The Circular Economy stimulates economic growth through efficient resource utilization.

The Circular Economy is expected to become increasingly crucial for achieving a sustainable society in the future.

Now, as we continue with food production, it is imperative to reduce the consumption of resources and energy, such as soil, fertilizers, and water.

Additionally, we should consider the costs associated with each element and the relationships between them. In other words, there are trade-offs between costs, conversion of costs to value, offsetting costs with other costs, and cost synergies.

For example, by adopting MBT Sustainable Cycle, the cost of waste disposal is absorbed by compost manufacturing costs. The cost of restoring degraded soil using MBT55 reduces the costs of compost and fertilizers. Functional agricultural products cultivated using MBT compost and fertilizers reduce food waste by over 20% and further cut medical expenses by over 20%. Moreover, the same manufacturing process allows the production of products in a different field, such as probiotics.

In the construction of a Circular Model, the concept of "Circular Model + Cost Absorption, Conversion to Value" is exceptionally vital. Furthermore, the process of compost and fertilizer usage leads to carbon fixation, resulting in income from carbon credits and realizing improved productivity.

**General composting period**
The composting period is the duration until the temperature stops rising even after turning. The approximate guidelines for the composting period are about two months for livestock manure in a deposition method, approximately three months for a mixture of crop residues and livestock manure, and more than six months for a mixture with woody materials.

**MBT Sustainable Cycle**
In contrast, the MBT Sustainable Cycle can completely decompose food waste and livestock excrement within 24 hours. With an MBT reactor, the cost is incurred only for the organic matter of fermentation accelerators and the electricity cost for 24 hours. This results in the elimination of odor and environmental issues associated with the processing of food waste and livestock excrement, reducing labor costs for 3 to 6 months. Additionally, returning a large amount of organic carbon to the soil contributes to mitigating global warming.
