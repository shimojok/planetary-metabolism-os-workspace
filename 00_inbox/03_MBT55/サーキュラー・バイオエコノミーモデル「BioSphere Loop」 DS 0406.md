### **BioSphere Loop Project**
**"MBT Sustainable Cycle Creating a Circular Bioeconomy"**  

**Objective:**  
A project to produce humus substances (MBT compost) and functional feed through decomposition and fermentation using MBT55.  

### **Core Initiatives**  
1. **Production of High-Efficiency Functional Fertilizers**  
   - Convert food waste (typically incinerated or landfilled) into compost within 24 hours, creating productivity-enhancing fertilizers.  

2. **Soil Restoration and Carbon Credit Revenue**  
   - Repair degraded soils using highly functional MBT humus materials, improving productivity while generating long-term carbon sequestration and carbon credit income.  

3. **Functional Feed to Prevent Infections and Enhance Growth**  
   - Produce purpose-specific functional feed by selectively utilizing nutrients from sorted food waste.  

4. **Livestock and Aquaculture Probiotics for Health Improvement**  
   - Develop probiotics that enhance animal health and growth performance.  

5. **Human-Grade Probiotics from Premium Ingredients**  
   - Manufacture probiotics for human use using rigorously selected raw materials.  

6. **Herbal Probiotics for Humans and Livestock**  
   - Create probiotics integrated with traditional herbal ingredients.  

7. **MBT Sustainable Cycle Network**  
   - Connect producers (agriculture, livestock, aquaculture, forestry) with food/processing companies to convert waste management costs into functional material production costs, eliminating incineration/landfilling, reducing CO2 emissions, and establishing a value-creating resource model.  

8. **MBT Reactor Subscription Model**  
   - Provide MBT reactors (specialized fermentation systems) to food manufacturers, distributors, and agricultural sectors via subscription.  

9. **Carbon Credit Management System**  
   - Implement a carbon credit tracking and revenue-sharing system integrated into MBT reactors.  

### **Technical Overview of MBT55**  
- **Microbial Composition**:  
  120+ microbial strains categorized into 4 colonies:  
  - Starch-degrading  
  - Protein-degrading  
  - Lipid-degrading  
  - Cellulose-degrading  
  - Aerobic microbes (55%) + Anaerobic microbes (45%)  

- **Capabilities**:  
  - **24-hour decomposition** of all organic waste:  
    Food waste, seafood residues, plant debris, livestock manure, driftwood, marine sludge, sewage sludge, etc.  
  - **High-temperature fermentation** (90°C) to process even recalcitrant materials (e.g., driftwood, livestock waste).  
  - **Detoxification** of heavy metals and chemicals in sludge.  

- **Outputs**:  
  - **Humus**: Rapid soil restoration + long-term carbon sequestration.  
  - **Bioactive metabolites**: Enhance human/livestock health and reduce medical costs.
