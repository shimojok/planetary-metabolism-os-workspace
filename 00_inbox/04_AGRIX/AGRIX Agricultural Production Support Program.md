## AGRIX Platform: Agricultural Production Support Program with Advanced Analytical Techniques and AI Utilization

	April 10, 2024 by Kaz Shimojo

**1. Overview**

AGRIX Platform provides an agricultural production support program utilizing MBT55 with AI-driven advanced analytical techniques.

**2. Proposed Analytical Techniques**

**2.1 Soil Analysis**

* **Physical Analysis:**
    * Particle size distribution
    * Density
    * Water holding capacity
    * Permeability
    * Structure
* **Chemical Analysis:**
    * Major elements (N, P, K, Ca, Mg, S)
    * Micronutrients
    * Organic matter
    * Humus
    * pH
    * Electrical conductivity
* **Biological Analysis:**
    * Microbial community
    * DNA sequencing
    * Metagenomics
    * Enzyme activity
* **MBT55-related Indicators:**
    * MBT55 microbial biomarkers
    * MBT55 soil fertility improvement indicators
    * MBT55 soil microbial activity indicators

**2.2 Plant Phenotyping**

* **Image Analysis:**

    * Shape
    * Growth rate

* **Spectral Analysis:**
    * Photosynthesis
    * Nutritional status
    * Water stress
    * Carbon dioxide uptake
* **Physiological Indicators:**
    * Photosynthetic rate
    * Respiration rate
    * Transpiration rate
    * Cell membrane permeability

* **Elucidation of Plant-Soil Microbiome Interactions:**
    * Utilize metagenomics and metatranscriptomics to analyze plant-soil microbiome interactions in detail, aiming to improve agricultural productivity by promoting plant-microbe interactions.
* **Plant Gene Expression Analysis:**
    * Identify genes related to environmental stress tolerance and quality improvement through RNA sequencing, and utilize them for varietal improvement and cultivation technique development.
* **Agricultural Production Automation with AI:**
    * Combine robotics and AI to automate tasks such as sowing, fertilization, irrigation, and harvesting, aiming to improve agricultural production efficiency and address labor shortages.
