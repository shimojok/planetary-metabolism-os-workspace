**AGRIX Industry Standards**

AGRIX industry standards are based on the following five themes:

* **Agricultural production**
* **Supply chain**
* **Food security**
* **Environmental impact reduction**
* **Climate change**

The following items were identified for each theme:

**Agricultural production**

* **Crop and food type**
* **Production area**
* **Producer**
* **Harvest season**
* **Quality**
* **Nutritional value**
* **Pesticide and fertilizer use**
* **Soil condition**
* **Impact of climate change**

**Supply chain**

* **Distribution channels**
* **Storage method**
* **Waste disposal method**
* **Greenhouse gas emissions**

**Food security**

* **Stable food supply**
* **Reduction of food loss**
* **Promotion of sustainable food production**

**Environmental impact reduction**

* **Reduction of greenhouse gas emissions**
* **Reduction of pesticide and fertilizer use**
* **Promotion of resource circulation**

**Climate change**

* **Restoration of degraded soil**
* **Compost use and land management**
* **Soil organic matter and carbon content**
* **CDM/carbon credits**

By organizing and extracting common items from these items, we get the following:

* **Crop and food type**
* **Production area**
* **Producer**
* **Harvest season**
* **Quality**
* **Nutritional value**
* **Price**
* **Distribution channels**
* **Storage method**
* **Waste disposal method**
* **Greenhouse gas emissions**
* **Pesticide and fertilizer use**
* **Soil condition**
* **Impact of climate change**

By defining these items as industry standards, the following systematization and flow can be achieved.

**Systematization**

Each item is registered in a database. The database uses blockchain technology to enhance security and reliability.

**Flow**

Data for each item is collected and recorded in the process of distributing crops and food. These data can be tracked and verified using blockchain technology.

This industry standard is the foundation for AGRIX's business development. It is also expected to play an important role in promoting DX (digital transformation) in the agricultural and food industry as a whole.

**Specific use cases**

The following specific use cases can be considered for this industry standard:

* **Improvement of traceability of agricultural products and food**
* **Reduction of food loss**
* **Promotion of sustainable food production**
* **Reduction of environmental impact**
* **Contribution to climate change mitigation**

For example, to improve the traceability of agricultural products and food, it is necessary to collect and record information such as harvest location, producer, harvest season, quality, and price in the process of distributing agricultural products and food, and track and verify it using blockchain technology. To do this, industry standards can be used to collect and record information efficiently and uniformly.

Also, to reduce food loss, it is necessary to understand the balance between the demand and supply of agricultural products and food. To do this, it is necessary to collect and analyze information such as the quality, price, and distribution status of agricultural products and food. To do this, industry standards can make it easier to collect the necessary information.

In this way, industry standards have the potential to contribute to the solution of various challenges in the agricultural and food industry.
