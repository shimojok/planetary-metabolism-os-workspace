## AquaGrid Project Pitch Deck

### 4. Cross-Sectoral Synergies

- Enables integrated AquaTower buildings combining food, water, and energy independence
    
- Supports sustainable urban agriculture via MBT compost & fermentation technology
    
- Functions as resilient infrastructure in disaster scenarios
    
- Key asset for climate-adaptive urban planning in collaboration with international institutions
    

---

### 5. Summary

AquaGrid is a comprehensive water-energy-food infrastructure solution addressing climate-era urban, agricultural, and disaster-related challenges. It serves as a bridge between diverse investor sectors and next-generation customer needs in social capital development.
