### AgriCloud Project: 
### A Circular and Sustainable Approach to Food Production

#### Introduction

Food security is one of the most pressing challenges facing the world today. According to the United Nations, the global population is expected to reach 9.7 billion by 2050, and the demand for food will increase by 70%. https://ferret-plus.com/9463 However, the current food system is inefficient, wasteful, and harmful to the environment and human health. It is estimated that about one-third of the food produced globally is lost or wasted, while millions of people suffer from hunger and malnutrition. https://www.maff.go.jp/j/kanbo/smart/smart_agri_technology/smart_agri_catalog_hinmoku/pdf/smartagri_catalog_kaju-11.pdf Moreover, the food system contributes to about 25% of the global greenhouse gas emissions, and uses about 70% of the freshwater resources. https://www.agricloud.ro/en/home/ It also causes land degradation, biodiversity loss, water pollution, and antimicrobial resistance. https://www.smartlogic.jp/agriware/index.html

To address these challenges, there is a need for a radical transformation of the food system, based on the principles of circularity and sustainability. Circularity means minimizing the inputs and outputs of the food system, by reducing waste, recycling nutrients, and using renewable energy sources. Sustainability means ensuring the long-term viability of the food system, by balancing the environmental, social, and economic aspects of food production and consumption. https://www.smartlogic.jp/agriware/feature/index.html

The AgriCloud Project is an innovative initiative that aims to apply the concepbalancingts of circularity and sustainability to food production, using the latest advances in biotechnology, ecology, and information technology. The project involves the development and implementation of a cloud-based platform that connects and integrates various components of the food system, such as crops, livestock, aquaculture, and waste management. The platform enables the optimization of the use of resources, the enhancement of the quality and safety of food products, and the generation of value-added by-products. The project also involves the creation and dissemination of knowledge and best practices on circular and sustainable food production, through education, training, and outreach activities.

The AgriCloud Project is expected to contribute to the achievement of several Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), such as ending hunger, ensuring healthy lives, promoting inclusive and sustainable economic growth, ensuring sustainable consumption and production patterns, and combating climate change and its impacts. http://smartlogic.jp/

Components of the AgriCloud Project
The AgriCloud Project consists of four main components: AgriCloud, AgriWare, Fundamental Process in Ecology, and Biotechnology. Each component has a specific role and function in the circular and sustainable food system, as described below.

AgriCloud
AgriCloud is a cloud-based platform that monitors and manages the various elements of the food system, such as crops, livestock, aquaculture, and waste. AgriCloud collects and analyzes data from sensors, drones, satellites, and other sources, and provides real-time information and feedback to the users. AgriCloud also uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to optimize the production processes, such as irrigation, fertilization, pest control, harvesting, processing, and distribution. AgriCloud enables the users to access the platform from anywhere, using any device, and to share and exchange data and information with other stakeholders, such as farmers, processors, retailers, consumers, researchers, and policymakers. AgriCloud aims to improve the efficiency, productivity, profitability, and sustainability of the food system, by reducing the inputs and outputs, increasing the quality and safety, and enhancing the traceability and transparency. https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fevo.2019.00219/full

AgriWare
AgriWare is a suite of software applications that support the AgriCloud platform, and provide specific tools and functions for the users. AgriWare includes the following applications:

•  AgriSense: a sensor-based application that measures and monitors the environmental and biological parameters of the crops, livestock, aquaculture, and waste, such as temperature, humidity, soil moisture, pH, nutrient levels, biomass, growth, health, and behavior.

•  AgriDrone: a drone-based application that captures and processes aerial images and videos of the crops, livestock, aquaculture, and waste, and provides information on the spatial distribution, density, diversity, and dynamics of the food system.

•  AgriSat: a satellite-based application that accesses and utilizes the global satellite data and imagery of the food system, and provides information on the regional and global patterns, trends, and impacts of the food production and consumption.

•  AgriNet: a network-based application that connects and integrates the data and information from the AgriSense, AgriDrone, and AgriSat applications, and provides a comprehensive and holistic view of the food system.

•  AgriLearn: a learning-based application that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to analyze and optimize the data and information from the AgriNet application, and provides recommendations and feedback to the users on how to improve the production processes and outcomes.

•  AgriTrade: a trade-based application that facilitates and supports the marketing and distribution of the food products and by-products, and provides information on the supply and demand, prices, quality, and safety of the food system. https://books.google.com/books/about/Fundamental_Processes_in_Ecology.html?id=PCeQDwAAQBAJ

Fundamental Process in Ecology
Fundamental Process in Ecology is a scientific framework that guides and informs the design and implementation of the AgriCloud Project, based on the principles of ecology and systems thinking. Fundamental Process in Ecology identifies and describes the key ecological processes that are essential for the functioning and resilience of the food system, such as energy flow, nutrient cycling, population dynamics, community interactions, and ecosystem services. Fundamental Process in Ecology also recognizes and addresses the complex and dynamic interactions and feedbacks between the food system and the broader social and environmental systems, such as climate change, land use change, water scarcity, and human health. Fundamental Process in Ecology aims to enhance the understanding and appreciation of the ecological aspects of the food system, and to promote the adoption of ecological practices and solutions that support the circularity and sustainability of the food system. https://www.studyiq.com/articles/ecological-succession/

Biotechnology
Biotechnology is a technological component that enables and enhances the AgriCloud Project, based on the application of biological sciences and engineering. Biotechnology involves the manipulation and modification of living organisms and their components, such as genes, cells, tissues, and enzymes, to produce useful products and services. Biotechnology includes the following techniques and applications:

•  Genetic engineering: a technique that involves the insertion, deletion, or modification of genes in organisms, to create new or improved traits or functions, such as disease resistance, drought tolerance, nutrient enhancement, and biofuel production.

•  Tissue culture: a technique that involves the growth and multiplication of cells and tissues in artificial media, to produce clones or hybrids of organisms, such as plants, animals, and microorganisms.

•  Fermentation: a technique that involves the use of microorganisms, such as bacteria, yeast, and fungi, to convert organic substances into other products, such as ethanol, lactic acid, and cheese.

•  Bioremediation: a technique that involves the use of microorganisms, plants, or enzymes, to degrade or detoxify pollutants or contaminants, such as oil, pesticides, and heavy metals.

•  Bioinformatics: a technique that involves the use of computer software and databases, to store, analyze, and interpret the biological data and information, such as DNA sequences, gene expression, and protein structures. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biotechnology

MBT Sustainable Cycle
MBT Sustainable Cycleは、AgriCloud Projectの中核となるコンセプトであり、MBT発酵機という特殊な装置を使って、地域の廃棄物を処理し、堆肥や飼料やプロバイオティクスを生産する循環型のシステムです。MBT Sustainable Cycleは、以下のような特徴と成果を持ちます。

•  廃棄物の削減：MBT Sustainable Cycleは、地域の廃棄物（食品廃棄物や農業廃棄物や家畜廃棄物など）をMBT発酵機に投入し、24時間で5分の1の重量に減らします。MBT発酵機は、廃棄物に含まれる有機物を分解し、二酸化炭素や水や熱などの無害な物質に変換します。MBT Sustainable Cycleは、廃棄物の量や重量や臭気や病原菌などの問題を解決し、廃棄物処理のコストや環境負荷を削減します。

•  堆肥の生産：MBT Sustainable Cycleは、MBT発酵機によって、廃棄物から堆肥を生産します。MBT発酵機は、廃棄物に含まれる有機物を分解し、窒素やリンやカリウムなどの栄養素を含む堆肥に変換します。MBT発酵機は、堆肥にMBTという特殊な微生物を添加し、堆肥の品質や効果を向上させます。MBT Sustainable Cycleは、堆肥を農業生産者や流通や消費者などに提供し、肥料の需要やコストや環境負荷を削減します。

•  飼料の生産：MBT Sustainable Cycleは、MBT発酵機によって、廃棄物から飼料を生産します。MBT発酵機は、廃棄物に含まれる有機物を分解し、タンパク質や脂肪や炭水化物などの栄養素を含む飼料に変換します。MBT発酵機は、飼料にMBTという特殊な微生物を添加し、飼料の品質や効果を向上させます。MBT Sustainable Cycleは、飼料を畜産や養殖などの生産者に提供し、飼料の需要やコストや環境負荷を削減します。

•  プロバイオティクスの生産：MBT Sustainable Cycleは、MBT発酵機によって、廃棄物からプロバイオティクスを生産します。MBT発酵機は、廃棄物に含まれる有機物を分解し、乳酸菌やビフィズス菌などの有益な微生物を含むプロバイオティクスに変換します。MBT発酵機は、プロバイオティクスにMBTという特殊な微生物を添加し、プロバイオティクスの品質や効果を向上させます。MBT Sustainable Cycleは、プロバイオティクスを人間や動物に提供し、健康や生活の質を向上させます。

AgriCloud Projectの資料を拝見しました。AgriCloudは、農業におけるデータ収集、分析、活用を支援するクラウドサービスです。AgriCloudは、以下のような特徴と成果を持っています。

•  AgriCloudは、様々な種類のセンサーやカメラを使って、圃場の環境や作物の状態をリアルタイムでモニタリングします。これにより、農業者は、気象や土壌、病害虫などの情報を手元のスマートフォンやパソコンで確認できます。

•  AgriCloudは、収集したデータをクラウド上で分析し、最適な栽培管理や収穫時期を提案します。また、AIや機械学習を用いて、作物の品質や収量を予測し、農業者の収入を向上させます。

•  AgriCloudは、Fintechと連携して、農業者の資金調達や保険の申し込みを簡単にします。AgriCloudが提供するデータや予測をもとに、金融機関や保険会社は、農業者の信用やリスクを評価し、適切なサービスを提供します。

•  AgriCloudは、AgriWareというソフトウェアを使って、農業者が自分でデータを管理や分析できるようにします。AgriWareは、簡単な操作で、センサーやカメラの設定やデータのダウンロードやグラフ化ができます。また、AgriWareは、農業に関する最新の知識や情報を提供し、農業者のスキルアップを支援します。

•  AgriCloudは、Fundamental Process in Ecologyという理論に基づいて、農業の環境負荷を低減し、持続可能な循環型農業を実現します。AgriCloudは、エネルギーの効率的な利用や廃棄物の減量や再利用、生物多様性の保全などを目指します。

•  AgriCloudは、バイオテクノロジーを活用して、農業の生産性や品質を向上させます。AgriCloudは、遺伝子組み換えや組織培養などの技術を用いて、耐病性や耐乾性などの特性を持つ作物を開発します。また、AgriCloudは、微生物や酵素などの生物資源を利用して、農業に有用な物質やエネルギーを生産します。

•  AgriCloudは、養鶏、畜産、酪農、養殖などの畜産業にも適用できます。AgriCloudは、センサーやカメラを使って、家畜や水産動物の健康や成長をモニタリングし、最適な飼育管理や飼料配合を提案します。また、AgriCloudは、畜産業における環境問題や動物福祉の改善にも貢献します。

以上のように、AgriCloudは、農業におけるデータ収集、分析、活用を支援するクラウドサービスとして、農業者の収入や生活の向上、農業の環境負荷の低減、農業の生産性や品質の向上などの多様な効果をもたらします。AgriCloudは、農業の未来を切り開く革新的なプロジェクトです。
