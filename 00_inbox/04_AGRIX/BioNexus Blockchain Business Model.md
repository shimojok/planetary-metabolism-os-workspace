#### Utilization of Blockchain Technology and Financial Product/Fundraising Models in the AGRIX Platform

##### Introduction

Blockchain technology holds the potential to bring about significant changes in the financial services sector, particularly in eight areas known as the "Golden Eight."

##### Utilization of Blockchain Technology in the AGRIX Platform

The AGRIX Platform utilizes MBT functional compost produced through the MBT Sustainable Cycle to construct BioValley, a high-functioning agricultural production infrastructure, thereby enhancing harvest volume, quality, nutritional value, and freshness retention.

Additionally, waste reduction through composting/feed production and waste management, along with healthcare cost reduction, are also anticipated. By recording these facts on the blockchain ledger, the following benefits can be realized:

- **Enhanced information transparency:** It becomes possible to transparently disclose production histories of agricultural products and their environmental impacts, gaining consumer trust.
- **Enhanced traceability:** By making the distribution process of agricultural products traceable, it can contribute to preventing the circulation of counterfeit products and improving safety.
- **Sharing and utilization of data:** Sharing data related to agricultural production can lead to improved production efficiency and the creation of new business opportunities.
- **Utilization of smart contracts:** Automating the trading of agricultural products can lead to cost reduction and increased transaction security.

##### Financial Product/Fundraising Models

The following financial product/fundraising models leveraging the aforementioned benefits can be considered:

**1. Token issuance**

- Issuance of tokens backed by MBT functional compost or agricultural products produced in BioValley, sold to investors to raise funds.
- Token holders can use them for purchasing agricultural products or supporting producers.

**2. Supply chain finance**

- Providing loans to agricultural producers or food processors based on transaction data recorded on the blockchain ledger.
- Compared to traditional lending, it enables faster and lower-cost fundraising.

**3. Carbon credits**

- Issuing and selling carbon credits based on the CO2 emission reduction achieved through the use of MBT functional compost.
- Companies can purchase carbon credits to reduce their own CO2 emissions.

**4. P2P lending**

- Providing a platform for direct lending from individual investors to agricultural producers or food processors.
- Investors can expect higher returns, while producers can raise funds at lower costs.

##### Comments as Information Provider

As an information provider, attention must be paid to the following points:

- **Regulatory environment:** Compliance with the regulatory environments of each country is necessary when designing financial product/fundraising models.
- **Security:** While blockchain technology boasts high security, attention must also be paid to risks such as cyberattacks.
- **User interface:** Since blockchain technology may be challenging for the general population to use, providing a user-friendly interface is necessary.

By considering these points, the utilization of blockchain technology in the AGRIX Platform is expected to contribute to innovation in the agricultural sector and the realization of a sustainable society.

### AGRIX Project: Agri-Blockchain Service Business Model

##### Overview

The AGRIX Project aims to deploy an agri-blockchain service business that integrates agricultural production, food processing, waste management, agricultural product trading, financial services, food reserves, disaster relief, and production adjustments using blockchain technology.

##### Business Content

**1. Agricultural Production and Food Processing**
- Construction of a high-functioning agricultural production infrastructure, "BioValley," through the use of MBT functional compost
- Recording agricultural product production histories, environmental impacts, and quality information on the blockchain ledger
- Enhancement of traceability in food processing

**2. Waste Management**
- Processing food waste and livestock manure into MBT functional compost
- Waste reduction and resource cycling through composting and feed production
- Reduction of waste management costs and environmental impacts

**3. Agricultural Product Trading**
- Safe and secure transactions based on agricultural product production histories and quality information
- Cost reduction and increased transaction security through automation with smart contracts
- Stabilization of agricultural product prices and increase in producer profits

**4. Financial Services**
- Providing loans to agricultural producers and food processors
- Issuance and sale of carbon credits
- Fundraising through P2P lending
- Promotion of investment in the agricultural sector

**5. Food Reserves**
- Preparation of reserves for food shortages in times of disaster
- Efficient inventory management and distribution using blockchain ledger

**6. Disaster Relief**
- Procurement and transportation of support materials to disaster-affected farms
- Understanding the situation of disasters and streamlining support activities

**7. Production Adjustments**
- Production planning based on supply and demand balance of agricultural products
- Prevention of price decline due to overproduction

##### Platform

A blockchain-based platform with the following functions will support these businesses:

- **Data recording and management:** Recording and managing data related to agricultural production, food processing, waste management, and agricultural product trading
- **Traceability:** Tracking the distribution process of agricultural products
- **Smart contracts:** Automation of transactions
- **Financial services:** Loans, investments, carbon credits, etc.
- **Information sharing:** Information sharing among agricultural producers, food processors, consumers, etc.

##### Benefits

- **Sharing and utilization of data:** Improvement of production efficiency and creation of new business opportunities
