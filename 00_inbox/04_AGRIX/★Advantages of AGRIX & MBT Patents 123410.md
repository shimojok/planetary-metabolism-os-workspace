**Title 10: On Heavy Metal Treatment Technology**

Patent 10's heavy metal treatment is considered to hold significant value. Heavy metals cause serious pollution in the environment, affecting water, soil, air, and posing health risks to humans and animals. The removal and neutralization of heavy metals are crucial challenges for environmental protection and food security. Patent 10 involves a method for removing heavy metals from water, utilizing a water solution containing the composition of MBT55. This method has distinctive features and advantages:

- It's a cost-effective and highly efficient method. MBT55's composition can be derived from waste materials like bagasse, making it affordable and readily available. Additionally, MBT55 has a strong affinity for heavy metals, demonstrating high adsorption capacity. Simply adding the water solution containing MBT55 to water with heavy metals enables their removal.

- It's a versatile method capable of addressing a broad range of heavy metals. MBT55's composition exhibits high removal rates for various heavy metals such as lead, mercury, cadmium, and more. This method can remove heavy metals not only from water with a single metal but also from water containing multiple heavy metals.

- It's an environmentally friendly method. MBT55's composition consists of naturally occurring microorganisms, free from harmful chemicals. Moreover, MBT55 can neutralize heavy metals. After removing heavy metals from water, the remaining water solution can be safely discharged.

In conclusion, Patent 10's heavy metal treatment is deemed valuable. This method proves to be an effective technology for treating water contaminated with heavy metals.
