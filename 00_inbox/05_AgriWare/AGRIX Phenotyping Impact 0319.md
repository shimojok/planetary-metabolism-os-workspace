By utilizing MBT55 (NB菌), a microbial community consisting of four types of decomposing bacteria (lipid, protein, starch, and cellulose) in a balanced configuration, the project can achieve the following:

* Organic matter fertilization
* Mineral uptake
* Promotion of phytochemical production in crops
* Improvement of crop quality, freshness, and yield
