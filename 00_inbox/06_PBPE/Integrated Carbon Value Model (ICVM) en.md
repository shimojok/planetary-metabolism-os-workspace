🧬 2. Definition of C⁺ Credit

🔹 C⁺ Credit = Multi-Value Carbon Unit

| Axis | Indicator | Conversion | Monetization |
|------|-----------|------------|--------------|
| Carbon Sequestration | tCO₂ fixed | 1tCO₂ = 1 C⁺ | Soil, compost, biomass analysis |
| Soil Regeneration | Humus Index | +10% = 0.2 C⁺ | MBT activity, humic substance generation |
| Food Quality | Polyphenol Index | +20% = 0.3 C⁺ | Nutritional value, antioxidant capacity |
| Freshness Retention | Freshness Score | +30% = 0.5 C⁺ | Shelf life, spoilage reduction |
| Food Loss Reduction | kg/year | 100kg = 0.4 C⁺ | Waste reduction × CO₂ equivalent |
| Healthcare Cost Reduction | $/year | $100 = 0.5 C⁺ | Disease risk reduction × medical cost data |
| Resource Monetization | $/ton | $200 = 1 C⁺ | Compost, fertilizer, bioenergy sales |

> 💡 Total value determines the number of C⁺ credits issued per project.

---

🧠 3. Evaluation Algorithm

🔸 ACAIN Nexus Engine (Strategic Layer)

- Input: Environmental, nutritional, quality, carbon data from AGRIX
- Process: Multi-axial scoring → weighted integration → credit valuation
- Output: Number of C⁺ credits, impact evaluation, financial value

🔸 Example Formula:

`math
C⁺ = α₁·CO₂ + α₂·Humus + α₃·Quality + α₄·Freshness + α₅·LossReduction + α₆·HealthImpact + α₇·BioRevenue
`

- α₁–α₇: Weighting coefficients adjustable by region, policy, investor priorities

---

🔗 4. Credit Flow & Integration

🔹 ESG/SDGs Investment Integration (ACAIN)

- Connect C⁺ credits to ESG funds and SDGs-aligned projects
- Provide impact evaluation reports to financial institutions, governments, global entities

🔹 Regional Currency & Subsidy Linkage (ANE)

- Return C⁺ credits to local farmers via subsidies or tax incentives
- Support resource monetization through MBT agricultural engines

🔹 International Carbon Market Proposal (ACAIN)

- Position C⁺ credits as a new framework beyond offset-based models
- Promote value-creating carbon credits in global climate finance
