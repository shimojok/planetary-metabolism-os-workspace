# AQLA Intelligence: The Brain Layer (MI)

AQLA Intelligence transforms raw metabolic data into actionable insights for both planetary health and human longevity. It functions as the predictive "Metabolic Brain" of the OS.

Unlike traditional correlation-based AI, our **Causal AI** processes the Planetary-Metabolic-Human (PMH) function:
$$Y = f(S, M, C, T)$$

### 2. MBT Metabolism Library
A graph database mapping the secondary metabolites produced by MBT55-fortified soil to human clinical biomarkers.
- **Reference:** Traditional Medicine (Kanpo) metabolic pathways.
- **Digital Twin:** Modeling the interaction between soil microbes, plant phytonutrients, and human gut health.

### 3. Food Is Medicine (FIM) Engine
AQLA OS automates the logic of "Preventive Healthcare" through data:
- **Input:** Soil Microbial Diversity Index (SMDI) + Human health data (via Fitbit/Google Health/Azure Health).
- **Process:** Causal AI identifies the specific nutrient gap required to repair human metabolism at the **"Mibyou" (pre-disease)** stage.
- **Output:** Precise prescription of "Metabolic Probiotics" or specific crop recommendations to restore human and soil vitality.

### なぜこれが「決定版」なのか

- 数式 (
    
    $$Y = f(S, M, C, T)$$
    
    ): 前回のホワイトペーパーとの一貫性を持たせています。
    
- **具体的用語**: 「未病（Mibyou）」「漢方（Kanpo）」といった下條様の強みと、「Google Health/Fitbit」という相手（Google/Microsoft）の強みを融合させています。
    
- **役割の明確化**: 生成AI（Generative AI）ではなく、因果推論AI（Causal AI）であることを強調し、技術的な「格」を上げています。
