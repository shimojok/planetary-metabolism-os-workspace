## **12. Expanding Amazon’s Global Influence Through Policy Collaboration**

**Theme: Building a Sustainable Food System and Ensuring Food Security ➡ Amazon’s Leadership in Multilateral Cooperation and SDGs**  

Through our collaboration with BioNexus, we will advance the following strategies to expand Amazon’s global policy influence:  

---  

### **🌍 1. Building Food Security Through Multilateral Cooperation (FAO, World Bank, Rockefeller Foundation)**  
- **Data-Driven Agricultural Platform Development**  
  Integrate Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud technology with BioNexus’ agricultural biotechnology to create a platform combining **real-time weather data, soil analysis, and crop prediction tools**. Partner with FAO and the World Bank to deploy this in developing nations.  
  → **Targets**: 20% improvement in food production efficiency and 30% reduction in waste.  

- **Small-Scale Farmer Empowerment Program**  
  Leverage the Rockefeller Foundation’s funding network to promote sustainable farming technologies (e.g., drought-resistant seeds, vertical farming). Utilize Amazon’s logistics network for direct farm-to-market distribution, aiming for **50% income growth for farmers**.  

---  

### **🌱 2. Amazon’s Leadership Strategy for Achieving UN SDGs**  
- **Establishing an "SDG-Aligned Supply Chain"**  
  Integrate BioNexus’ sustainability AI into Amazon’s procurement systems. Develop transaction criteria requiring **zero deforestation, water resource protection, and fair labor practices**, applying these standards to all suppliers by 2030.  
  → Directly aligns with SDG2 (Zero Hunger) and SDG12 (Responsible Consumption).  

- **Global Education and Innovation Hubs: "Amazon Green Hub"**  
  Launch research facilities in Africa and Southeast Asia. Provide training in biotech and digital agriculture to young entrepreneurs and scientists, fostering **100,000 next-generation sustainability leaders**.  

---  

### **🌏 3. Participation in Global Climate Action Frameworks**  
- **"Carbon-Negative Supply Chain" Pledge**  
  Exceed Paris Agreement goals by achieving a **carbon-negative supply chain by 2030**. Utilize BioNexus’ **soil carbon sequestration technology** to maximize CO2 absorption in agriculture.  

- **Global Climate Fund Initiative**  
  Partner with the World Bank and EU to establish a fund focused on climate adaptation (e.g., flood prediction systems, salt-tolerant crops). Amazon commits **$1 billion** in seed funding to model public-private collaboration.
