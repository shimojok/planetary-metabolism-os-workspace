## **Responding to Thomas Piketty’s Warning:  
How MBT55 / MBT Sustainable Cycle and the AGRIX Platform Build the Next Natural Capital Infrastructure**

---

### **Introduction: The Climate Crisis Is an Economic Crisis**

Climate change is not only an environmental issue.  
It is a **structural failure of our economic system**.

Thomas Piketty, author of *Capital in the 21st Century*, has repeatedly emphasized:

> “Climate change and inequality are two sides of the same structural problem.”

In other words, the climate crisis is not caused by a lack of technology.  
It is caused by **institutions that fail to value natural capital**.

---

## **1. Piketty’s Three Core Concerns**

### **1. Natural capital is invisible**  
Ecosystem services are not reflected in economic systems, accelerating degradation.

### **2. Public investment is insufficient**  
Climate adaptation and circular infrastructure remain underdeveloped.

### **3. Inequality amplifies environmental damage**  
Overconsumption by the wealthy and vulnerability of the poor reinforce each other.

Piketty calls for **institutional reform and renewed public investment**.

---

## **2. MBT55 / MBT Sustainable Cycle and AGRIX: A Practical Implementation of Piketty’s Vision**

Piketty’s diagnosis is powerful.  
But solving the problem requires more than policy—it requires **infrastructure**.

This is where MBT55 and the AGRIX Platform come in.

---

### **① AGRIX Platform: Making Natural Capital Visible and Investable**

AGRIX enables:  
- Measurement of soil, water, and ecosystem services  
- Digitalization and verification  
- Financialization (credits, tokens)  
- Infrastructure-level management  

This is the **public capital foundation** Piketty argues is essential for climate action.

---

### **② MBT55: A Distributed Circular Infrastructure for Local Regeneration**

MBT55 / MBT Sustainable Cycle provides:  
- Waste-to-resource conversion  
- Closed-loop carbon–nitrogen–phosphorus cycles  
- Deployable, decentralized infrastructure  

This addresses the “lack of public investment” Piketty highlights—  
but through **scalable, private-sector-driven circular infrastructure**.

---

### **③ AGRIX × MBT: Solving Inequality and Ecological Crisis Together**

Together, they:  
- Empower smallholders and rural communities  
- Create local employment  
- Restore ecosystems  
- Reduce climate vulnerability  

This aligns directly with Piketty’s argument that  
**inequality and ecological crisis must be solved simultaneously**.

---

## **3. The Future of Natural Capital Infrastructure**

The next economic infrastructure must be built on natural capital.  
To achieve this, we need:

- Standardized natural capital measurement  
- Investment in distributed circular systems  
- Community-level value creation  
- Alignment with public policy  

MBT55 and AGRIX provide both the **technology** and the **institutional architecture** to make this possible.

---

## **Conclusion: Turning Piketty’s Theory into Reality**

Piketty’s vision of  
**“a sustainable and equitable form of capitalism”**  
cannot be achieved through policy alone.

It requires infrastructure that can:  
- Regenerate natural capital  
- Circulate resources  
- Create equitable value  
- Strengthen communities  

MBT55 and the AGRIX Platform offer a practical, scalable pathway to that future.

- 未来の自然資本の価値が過小評価され  
- 気候対策が後回しになり  
- 結果として環境劣化が加速する  

という構造的問題が生じます。

---

ピケティは、気候変動対策には **大規模な公共投資** が不可欠だと述べています。

国家レベルの資本形成が必要だと強調します。

---

## **③ 資本主義の構造的問題と環境危機**

ピケティは、不平等と環境危機は構造的に結びついていると指摘します。

- 高所得層の過剰消費  
- 低所得層の脆弱性  
- 自然資本の劣化  
- 地域格差の拡大  

これらは同じメカニズムから生まれており、  
**不平等を是正しなければ環境危機も解決しない**という立場です。
