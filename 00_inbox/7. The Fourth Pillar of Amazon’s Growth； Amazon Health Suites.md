## **7. The Fourth Pillar of Amazon’s Growth； Amazon Health Suites**
  
  **～A Data-Driven Healthcare Ecosystem Pioneering "Health Capitalism"～**

#### Health is a catalyst for economic growth and the foundation for productivity improvement.

According to a McKinsey report, improving health not only enhances individuals' quality of life but also significantly boosts national productivity. However, while advancements in medical technology have extended life expectancy, the increasing prevalence of lifestyle-related diseases and chronic health issues has become a serious concern, exacerbating the financial strain on healthcare systems worldwide. In today’s society, burdened by challenges such as climate change, comprehensive solutions driven by innovation are more critical than ever.

Extending healthy life expectancy is key to improving productivity for all. Extending healthy life expectancy directly addresses the economic and social challenges posed by aging populations. Increasing the proportion of healthy workers and reducing healthcare costs form the foundation for sustainable economic growth. 

According to estimates in Japan, it is possible to reduce the health burden by 30% by 2040, with an estimated GDP impact of $538 billion. Achieving this requires a focus on preventive medicine and personalized health management. This necessity points to the need for a comprehensive platform that not only advances medical technology but also streamlines healthcare and holistically supports individual health.
