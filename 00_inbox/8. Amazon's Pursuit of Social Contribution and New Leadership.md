## **8. Amazon's Pursuit of Social Contribution and New Leadership**

**Theme: Improving Food Security and Advancing Sustainable Agriculture in Developing Countries such as Africa**

Amazon should not only be a business leader but also a global pioneer in social contribution and sustainable development. This initiative is built on three main pillars:
#### 1. Realizing Sustainable Agriculture and a Green Food System

BioValley × Amazon Food Business:
By leveraging BioNexus’ BioValley network, Amazon will establish a sustainable agricultural model. This approach eliminates dependency on chemical fertilizers, creating an environmentally friendly production system that strengthens food security and enhances responsiveness to climate change.

#### 2. Promoting Food Loss Reduction and Fair-Trade Manufacturing

We Farm with Amazon:
By implementing advanced recycling technologies to convert food waste and agricultural residues into valuable resources, Amazon can drastically reduce food loss and build a sustainable production and distribution system. Additionally, promoting fair trade across the supply chain will enhance transparency and uphold social responsibility.

#### 3. Contributing to Economic Development in Developing Countries and Innovating Agriculture in Africa

Local Adoption to Boost Agricultural Productivity:
Introducing BioNexus’ cutting-edge agricultural technologies into developing regions such as Africa and Southeast Asia will significantly improve local agricultural productivity and support the economic independence of farmers. This effort will improve food security, create employment opportunities, and stimulate regional economic growth.

**Overall Aim:**
Through the integration of BioNexus technologies, Amazon will build a sustainable food supply system that not only reduces environmental impact but also supports agricultural development and economic self-reliance in developing countries. In doing so, Amazon will solidify its position as a global leader committed to both climate action and social contribution, thereby creating a new growth engine driven by sustainability.
