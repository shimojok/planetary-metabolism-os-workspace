# 提案書：気候変動と生物多様性危機に対するMBT55とAquaGridの戦略的ソリューション

## 背景

近年、気候変動による極端気象現象（大型台風、洪水、熱波、干ばつ、森林火災など）が世界各地で深刻化し、社会的・経済的損失が拡大しています。特に都市部や農村地域では、水害や水不足が同時に進行する「水のパラドックス」が発生し、社会インフラの脆弱性が顕在化しています。

AXAが提唱する「Climate Change & Biodiversity」戦略は、脱炭素投資、生物多様性保全、自然資本会計などを通じたリスク回避と将来世代への責任ある対応に注力しています。しかし、AXAが危機意識を共有するこれらの課題に対し、我々は実装可能な具体的かつ革新的ソリューションを保有しています。

## 提案技術：MBT55とAquaGrid

### 1. MBT55による森林火災・土壌再生・炭素隔離ソリューション

MBT55は、都市ごみ・バイオ廃棄物などを発酵処理し、高機能な腐植質（MBT堆肥）を生成する独自技術です。この腐植質は以下の環境価値を生み出します：

- 土壌の保水性と耐火性の向上 → 森林火災の発生を大幅に抑制
    
- 地表温度の安定化 → 局地的気候の改善と生物多様性の維持
    
- 炭素の長期固定 → 炭素隔離によるクレジット創出
    
- 汚染土壌や海底堆積物の分解・浄化 → 自然回復の促進
    

### 2. AquaGridによる多目的水資源・エネルギーインフラ

AquaGridは、都市部や農村部において地下に大規模な貯水・導水路を整備し、次のような多機能インフラを提供する構想です：

- 洪水対策：大雨時に水を一時貯留し、流出量を制御
    
- 農業用水：乾季に貯水を活用し、持続的農業を支援
    
- 水力発電：落差を利用したマイクロ水力発電による再生可能エネルギー供給
    
- 公共投資による地域雇用創出とインフラ強靭化

# Proposal: MBT55 and AquaGrid as Strategic Solutions for Climate Change and Biodiversity Crises

## Background

In recent years, extreme weather events caused by climate change—such as powerful typhoons, floods, heatwaves, droughts, and wildfires—have increased in frequency and intensity worldwide, leading to significant social and economic losses. Both urban and rural areas are experiencing a "water paradox," where water-related disasters and water shortages occur simultaneously, exposing the vulnerability of existing infrastructure.

AXA's "Climate Change & Biodiversity" strategy emphasizes risk reduction and responsible action for future generations through decarbonization, biodiversity conservation, and natural capital accounting. We fully share this sense of urgency and offer tangible, scalable, and innovative solutions to these shared challenges.

## Proposed Technologies: MBT55 and AquaGrid

### 1. MBT55: Wildfire Prevention, Soil Regeneration, and Carbon Sequestration

MBT55 is a proprietary technology that transforms municipal and bio-waste into high-functional humus (MBT compost) through microbial fermentation. This humus contributes to environmental resilience in the following ways:

- Improves soil water retention and fire resistance → significantly reduces wildfire risk
    
- Stabilizes surface temperatures → supports local climate moderation and biodiversity
    
- Fixes carbon over the long term → enables generation of carbon credits
    
- Decomposes and detoxifies polluted soils and seabed sediments → facilitates ecological recovery
    

### 2. AquaGrid: Multipurpose Water and Energy Infrastructure

AquaGrid is a comprehensive infrastructure concept that installs large-scale underground water storage and flow channels in urban and rural areas, enabling the following functions:

- Flood control: temporarily stores excess rainfall and regulates runoff
    
- Agricultural irrigation: secures sustainable water use during dry seasons
    
- Micro-hydropower: utilizes water drop for renewable energy generation
    
- Regional job creation and infrastructure resilience through public investment
    

## Strategic Significance for AXA

AXA emphasizes investment in disaster prevention, climate adaptation, and societal resilience. Our proposal aligns with AXA’s priorities in the following key areas:

- **Risk Reduction**: mitigates insurance claim risks from wildfires and floods
    
- **Carbon & Biodiversity Credits**: synergizes with AXA's green finance strategy
    
- **Nature-Based Solutions (NbS)**: strengthens AXA’s NbS investment portfolio
    
- **Emerging Market Expansion**: supports ESG-driven growth and social impact
    

## Future Collaboration

By combining AXA’s global financial capacity and influence with our technology and on-the-ground implementation capabilities, we can dramatically enhance climate resilience in both urban and rural contexts and create meaningful progress in addressing global climate and biodiversity crises.
