2023ワークショップ「レジリエントな未来のための食料システムの資金調達」で講演し、](https://www.linkedin.com/company/finance-in-common?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)[米州開発銀行](https://www.linkedin.com/company/inter-american-development-bank?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)と[国際農業開発基金（IFAD）](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ifad?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)の後援による報告書を発表することができて光栄でした。報告書で概説された勧告は、より強靱な食料システムの構築において多国間開発銀行と国家開発銀行間の協力を改善することを目的としている。 [#G20](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/g20?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) MDB の自己資本比率改革により、[#SDG](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/sdg?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)資金のギャップを埋める期待が高まります。[#foodsystems](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/foodsystems?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)への投資対象を絞り、財務コストの上昇に対処する方法に大きな変化はまだありません[](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/foodsystems?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)最も必要とされている国に投資を行うと、不平等と持続不可能な土地利用慣行が永続する可能性があります。モメンタス[・グローバル](https://www.linkedin.com/company/momentus-global?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text)ワーキングペーパーは 信頼できる財務上の意思決定を行うには、透明性のある局所的なリスク評価を可能にするデジタル変革が必要です。✔ 5. 気候適応と回復力のための革新: ベストプラクティスを採用し、NDB 12h  Edited

I had the pleasure to speak at the [Finance In Common](https://www.linkedin.com/company/finance-in-common?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) 2023 Workshop, Financing Food Systems for Resilient Futures, and launch a report sponsored by [Inter-American Development Bank](https://www.linkedin.com/company/inter-american-development-bank?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) and [International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ifad?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text). The recommendations outlined in the report aim to improve collaboration between Multilateral and National Development Banks in building more resilient food systems. [#G20](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/g20?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) MDB capital adequacy reforms raise hope of closing the [#SDG](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/sdg?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) finance gap. Still without transformational change in how we target investments and address rising finance costs for [#foodsystems](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/foodsystems?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) investments in the countries of greatest need, we are likely to to perpetuate inequalities and unsustainable land use practices.   The [Momentus Global](https://www.linkedin.com/company/momentus-global?trk=feed-detail_main-feed-card-text) working paper is open for comment until September 30th and seeks input on the transformative recommendations to: ✔ 1. Make Food Systems a Policy Imperative: It's about time MDBs align with NDBs to spur government policies related to food systems. ✔ 2.Structure Globally, Invest Locally: While pooling funds is critical, the end goal should be to benefit local enterprises and contributors to the food system. NDBs play a pivotal role in directing global funds to grassroots levels. ✔ 3. Broker Innovative Finance: To nurture the food systems, diverse financial stakeholders need to come together. ✔ 4. Retool the Risk Function: To make credible financial decisions, we need a digital transformation that enables transparent and localized risk assessments. ✔ 5. Innovate for Climate Adaptation and Resilience: Adopting best practices and giving NDBs better access to climate finance resources can bolster our food systems against unforeseen adversities. You can read the paper here.
