### 🔹 生態学的ハイパーサイクルの再現

AGRIXの基盤となるMBT55発酵技術は、有機廃棄物を短時間で分解・腐植質化し、**炭素隔離・土壌改良・農業収量向上**を同時に実現する「MBT Sustainable Cycle」を構築します。これにより、都市と農村をつなぐ循環型インフラの構築も視野に入れております。

### 🔹 テクノロジーと金融の融合

### 🔹 サステナブル投資ネットワークと自然資本

ANE（農業自然経済圏）およびACAIN（農業炭素資産投資ネットワーク）では、再生型農業から生まれる自然資本や炭素クレジットの可視化とトークン化を進め、**気候ファイナンス**や**ESG投資**との接続を実現しております。

### 🔹 新興市場への直接的インパクト

アジア・アフリカ・ラテンアメリカなどの開発途上地域において、**気候変動と経済課題の同時解決**を図りつつ、農業従事者が自立的に持続可能な経済圏を構築できる仕組みを提供しています。

### ■ Message to Mr. Francisco Obreque (English Translation)

Dear Mr. Francisco Obreque,

I hope this message finds you well. Thank you again for your valuable insights and guidance during our previous exchanges.

I would like to take this opportunity to share with you an update on a project we are currently advancing—**the AGRIX Project**—and humbly seek your perspective and potential interest.

This initiative is a comprehensive **sustainability platform** that integrates agriculture, environment, technology, and finance. It aligns closely with the World Bank's goals in **enhancing climate resilience, improving agricultural productivity, and promoting financial inclusion** in developing regions.

#### Reconstructing the Ecological Hypercycle

At the core of the AGRIX Project is the **MBT55 fermentation technology**, which enables the rapid decomposition and humification of organic waste. This creates the **MBT Sustainable Cycle**, capable of achieving **carbon sequestration, soil regeneration, and higher agricultural yields**, while contributing to the development of a circular infrastructure that links urban and rural systems.

#### Integration of Technology and Finance

The platform incorporates **AI and blockchain technologies** through systems such as AgriWare, AgriChain, and SafetyChain. These enable **integrated management of agricultural production, distribution, and access to finance**, with a special focus on improving **financial inclusion and transactional transparency for smallholder farmers**.

#### Sustainable Investment Network and Natural Capital

Our frameworks ANE (Agricultural Natural Economy) and ACAIN (Agricultural Carbon Asset Investment Network) support the **visualization and tokenization of natural and carbon assets** derived from regenerative agriculture. These tools connect farmers directly with **climate finance and ESG-aligned investment networks**.

#### Impact in Emerging Markets

In regions such as Asia, Africa, and Latin America, the project aims to address **climate and economic challenges in an integrated manner**, providing small-scale farmers with the means to establish **self-sustaining economic ecosystems**.

#### Scalability and Potential for Collaboration

While we are currently moving forward with pilot implementations, we are also exploring **strategic collaboration with international development finance institutions** for broader deployment. We believe that the expertise and global reach of the World Bank could greatly enhance the societal impact of this initiative.


If you find this of interest, I would be delighted to share with you the introductory materials of the AGRIX Project. I would greatly appreciate any feedback or thoughts you may have.

Thank you very much for your continued support and consideration.

Sincerely,  
Kaz Shimojo
BioNexus Holdings Co-Founder
