## 🌍 Impact Vision Map: AGRIX & MBT Sustainable Cycle for Carbon Neutral Future

### 1️⃣ Clothing Waste & Organic Cotton Initiative (CW&OC)

- **Challenge:** 92M tons clothing waste/year → 84.6M tons CO₂e emissions
- **Solution:**
    - Convert clothing waste → carbonized boards (disaster housing)
    - MBT fertilizers for African organic cotton
- **Impact:**
    - 13.6M tons CO₂/year reduction (16% of apparel emissions)
    - Carbon-negative building boards
    - Jobs & women’s empowerment in Africa

### 2️⃣ MBT Sustainable Cycle Carbon Neutral Model

- **Challenge:** Agricultural waste, food loss, chemical fertilizer dependency
- **Solution:**
    - MBT55 fermentation → organic fertilizers & humic substances
    - Food production support & food loss reduction
    - MBT Probiotics for preventive healthcare
- **Impact:**
    - Waste → resources, CO₂ sequestration
    - Improved soil fertility, higher yields, hunger reduction
    - Lower healthcare costs via preventive nutrition
    - Corporate participation through carbon credit purchase

### 3️⃣ MBT Food & Herbal Probiotics Model

- **Challenge:** Food waste, limited efficacy understanding of herbal medicine
- **Solution:**
    - Food waste → high-value probiotics (e.g., reCLA for gut health)
    - Herbal Probiotics (next-gen traditional medicine) via HealthBook analysis
- **Impact:**
    - Waste reduction + CO₂ avoidance
    - Preventive healthcare for women & children
    - Development of post-herbal medicine market
