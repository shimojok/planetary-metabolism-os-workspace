These initiatives include:  
AGRIX, HealthBook, MBT Food & Herbal Probiotics, AquaTower, AquaGrid Project, and AquaMake.

My goal is to localize these projects and make you the protagonists—empowering you to lead the way in achieving carbon neutrality and solving food system challenges.
