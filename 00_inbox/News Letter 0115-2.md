Why MBT Agricultural Products Enable Price Reduction and Stability

Conventional agriculture is fundamentally externally dependent, with prices heavily influenced by crude oil costs and geopolitical risks—particularly through imported fertilizers.
In contrast, MBT agricultural products operate on a localized circular model, making structural cost reduction and price stability possible.

Dramatic Reduction in Production Costs (Supply-Side Logic)

• Fertilizer and agrochemical costs (-80%)
Through the MBT Sustainable Cycle, functional compost is produced locally from regional organic waste, eliminating dependence on imported chemical fertilizers.

• Improved yields
Built-in biosecurity functions suppress pests and diseases, minimizing the risk of crop failure and unharvestable losses.

Lower Distribution and Waste Costs (Distribution-Side Logic)

• Extended freshness and shelf life
Enhanced antioxidant capacity (polyphenols and flavonoids) slows spoilage during transportation, retail, and at home. A 30% reduction in food loss is effectively returned to consumers as a lower cost per edible unit.
