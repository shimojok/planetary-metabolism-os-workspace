## Nigeria Development Plan × AGRIX Projec

### Overview of BioNexus Holdings

[[ナイジェリア開発計画✕AGRIX Project 0718]]

BioNexus Holdings aims to introduce the AGRIX Platform as a company providing agri-information and financial services. The goal is to transform the global food system, restore degraded soils, and tackle climate change. At the heart of this project is the "MBT Sustainable Cycle," which aims to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability in Africa.

### Key Components of the AGRIX Platform

1. **AgriWare**:
   - Provides MBT technology information and cultivation management systems to support local farmers in growing crops efficiently and effectively.

2. **SafetyChain**:
   - Ensures the safety and traceability of agricultural production, guaranteeing the safety of food products consumed and enhancing quality control throughout the distribution process.

3. **AgriChain/World Foods Open Market**:
   - Supports the distribution and trading of agricultural products in the global market, increasing income for local farmers by enabling their products to reach international consumers.

### Core Technology - MBT Sustainable Cycle

#### MBT55 (NB Bacteria) and MBT Fermentation Machine
- **Function**:
  - Converts food waste and livestock excrement into functional compost and feed within 24 hours.

- **Benefits**:
  1. **Reduction of waste disposal costs**: Significantly lowers costs through waste recycling.
  2. **Production of high-quality, domestically produced functional compost**: Reduces reliance on imported fertilizers by producing locally.
  3. **Improvement of agricultural productivity in Africa**: Enhances soil quality, leading to increased crop yields.
  4. **Increase in agricultural income and improvement in food security**: Higher productivity leads to increased income for farmers and improved food security.
  5. **Reduction of food loss by over 30% through improved freshness retention**: Utilizing high-quality compost enhances crop freshness and reduces food loss.

### BioValley Network

- Establishes 3,000 BioValley sites across Africa, focusing on Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa.
- Supports seamless agribusiness from procurement to production, processing, trading, distribution, and collection.

### Collaboration between BioNexus and Amazon

- **Construction of AGRIX Platform on AWS**:
  - The AGRIX platform is built on Amazon AWS, providing an efficient and scalable system.

- **Role of BioValley**:
  - Each BioValley functions as Amazon’s food fulfillment center, strengthening Amazon's food business and contributing to food security in Africa.

- **Sales Channels**:
  - Agricultural products are sold on Amazon’s online marketplace, providing access to global customers.

This plan is expected to significantly enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability in Africa, contributing to local economic development. By offering competitive products in the global market as a local company, this project has the potential to make a substantial impact on agriculture and the economy in Nigeria and across Africa.
