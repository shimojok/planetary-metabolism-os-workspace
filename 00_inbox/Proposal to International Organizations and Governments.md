### Proposal to International Organizations and Governments

1. **Convert Organic Waste into Resources and Economic Value**  
     → Food waste, livestock manure, sewage sludge, agricultural residues, etc.
    
2. **Restore Degraded Land with MBT Humus and Functional Compost**  
     → Improve productivity, prevent desertification, and support reforestation
    
3. **Provide MBT-Based Functional Feed and Probiotics to Enhance Livestock and Aquaculture Health**  
     → Reduce the use of antibiotics and environmental load
    
4. **Utilize the AGRIX Platform to Integrate Agricultural Production, Data, and Finance**  
     → Support climate-smart agriculture, local industry creation, and trade
    
5. **Develop a Global Carbon Credit Ecosystem Centered on MBT55**  
     → Establish international carbon measurement standards for soil, compost, and feed

### Toward the Future

The AGRIX Project aims not only to resolve environmental and food problems, but also to serve as a **new model for global economic circulation** through the realization of a **hypercycle economy** based on ecological principles.

At the heart of this system lies **ANE**, which organically integrates data, production, and finance, and **ACAIN**, which accelerates global deployment through international collaboration.

We propose a world where waste is transformed into value, and environmental solutions generate economic returns — a world where local action creates global impact.

The time has come to move beyond theory and act through concrete systems and technologies. With the MBT Sustainable Cycle, AGRIX Platform, and the ecosystem built by ANE and ACAIN, we are ready to start this journey.
