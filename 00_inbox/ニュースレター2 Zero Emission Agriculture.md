---

MBT Sustainable Cycleによる土壌炭素隔離の仕組み

AGRIXの戦略の中核をなすのが、MBT Sustainable Cycleです。これは、土壌中に炭素を効率的に隔離し、長期的な環境保全を可能にする技術です。農業現場で排出される二酸化炭素を、再生可能な方法で土に戻すことで、炭素循環の新たなサイクルを確立。これにより、持続可能な農業経営が実現されるだけでなく、土壌の肥沃度向上にも寄与します。


---

炭素クレジット市場と農業の新たな収益モデル

炭素クレジット市場の拡大は、従来の農業経営に新たな収益源をもたらします。AGRIXは、炭素隔離の実績を数値化し、クレジットとして市場に売却することで、農家や投資家に対して魅力的な経済的インセンティブを提供しています。これにより、環境保全と経済成長の両立が現実のものとなり、農業が単なる生産産業を超えて、気候変動対策の重要な一翼を担うことになります。


---

ESG投資家が注目するサステナブルアグリビジネス

持続可能な農業は、ESG（環境・社会・ガバナンス）投資家にとっても大きな魅力です。AGRIXの取り組みは、環境配慮型のビジネスモデルとして国際的に高い評価を受けています。農業の未来を担う新たな収益モデルは、従来の化学肥料や大量生産の手法に依存しない、環境と共生する生産システムを確立し、持続可能な社会の実現に大きく貢献しています。

#### Zero Emission Agriculture — AGRIX’s Carbon Credit Strategy

**From an “Emission Industry” to a “Savior of Climate Change” — The Frontline of Returning Carbon to Soil**

In response to global warming, innovative initiatives in the agricultural sector are capturing attention. AGRIX is promoting “Zero Emission Agriculture” through its cutting-edge carbon credit strategy, which simultaneously reduces environmental impact and creates new revenue streams.

**Soil Carbon Sequestration with the MBT Sustainable Cycle**

At the heart of AGRIX’s strategy is the MBT Sustainable Cycle. This technology efficiently sequesters carbon in the soil, making long-term environmental conservation possible. By returning carbon dioxide, emitted from agricultural activities, back into the soil through renewable methods, a new cycle of carbon circulation is established. This not only enables sustainable farming practices but also contributes to improved soil fertility.

**Carbon Credit Market and a New Revenue Model in Agriculture**

The expansion of the carbon credit market offers a new revenue source for traditional farming practices. AGRIX quantifies its carbon sequestration achievements and sells these credits on the market, providing attractive economic incentives to farmers and investors. As a result, the balance between environmental protection and economic growth becomes a reality, positioning agriculture as a key player in combating climate change rather than merely a production industry.

**Sustainable Agribusiness That Attracts ESG Investors**

Sustainable agriculture is also highly attractive to ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investors. AGRIX’s initiatives have earned international recognition as an environmentally conscious business model. The new revenue model driving the future of agriculture is not dependent on conventional chemical fertilizers or mass-production methods; rather, it establishes a production system that coexists with the environment, significantly contributing to the realization of a sustainable society.

---

AGRIX’s Zero Emission Agriculture strategy goes beyond technological innovation—it aims for environmental preservation, economic development, and overall societal sustainability. With the advancement of soil carbon sequestration technology and the growth of the carbon credit market, global efforts to combat climate change are expected to be further accelerated.
