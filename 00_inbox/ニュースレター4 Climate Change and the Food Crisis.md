##### 🌱 AGRIXがもたらす変革

1️⃣ 気候変動が農業に与える影響とAGRIXの解決策

異常気象による作物の不作、土壌の劣化、農業資材の価格高騰は、世界中の農家にとって大きな課題です。AGRIXは、**「MBT Sustainable Cycle」**を活用し、気候変動の影響を抑えながら、生産性を向上させる技術を提供しています。

土壌の水分保持力向上：干ばつ・豪雨の影響を受けにくい農地を構築

生物多様性の回復：土壌微生物の活性化による健康な土壌環境の再生

持続可能な栽培モデル：従来の化学肥料に依存せず、高品質な農作物を生産


2️⃣ MBT55による土壌改良と炭素隔離の実績

MBT55は、土壌微生物の多様性を高め、炭素を土に戻す生態学的ハイパーサイクルを実現する技術です。すでに多くの地域で土壌の炭素貯留量を増加させ、収穫量向上を達成しています。

MBT55の効果

炭素隔離：農地に有機炭素を固定し、温室効果ガスを削減

肥料の内製化：堆肥の機能を向上させ、化学肥料の使用量を削減

土壌の再生：微生物の活性により栄養バランスを整え、劣化土壌を修復



3️⃣ 農業生産の未来を変える「BioValleyモデル」

AGRIXは、農業、畜産、食品加工、流通を一体化した「BioValleyモデル」を構築し、持続可能な農業経済を確立します。

農産物の高品質化：ポリフェノールや栄養価の高い作物の生産

食品ロス削減：MBT55堆肥を活用した鮮度保持力の向上

新しい収益モデル：炭素クレジットやサステナブル農業への投資機会の提供

### The AGRIX Project's Challenge to Address Climate Change and the Food Crisis

Climate change is causing droughts, heavy rains, and soil degradation, severely impacting global agricultural production. Traditional farming methods are struggling to cope, and sustainable solutions are urgently needed. The AGRIX Project takes on this challenge with innovative biotechnology and soil management techniques.


---

🌱 How AGRIX is Transforming Agriculture

1️⃣ The Impact of Climate Change on Agriculture and AGRIX’s Solutions

Extreme weather, declining soil quality, and rising agricultural input costs pose significant challenges for farmers worldwide. AGRIX utilizes the "MBT Sustainable Cycle" to mitigate the effects of climate change while enhancing productivity.

Improved soil water retention: Creating farmland that is more resilient to droughts and heavy rains

Restoration of biodiversity: Revitalizing soil ecosystems by activating microbial life

Sustainable farming model: Producing high-quality crops without reliance on chemical fertilizers


2️⃣ Proven Results of MBT55 in Soil Improvement and Carbon Sequestration

MBT55 enhances microbial diversity in the soil, achieving an ecological hypercycle that returns carbon to the land. It has already increased soil carbon storage and improved yields in multiple regions.

Effects of MBT55

Carbon sequestration: Fixing organic carbon in the soil to reduce greenhouse gases

Self-sufficient fertilization: Enhancing compost functionality and reducing chemical fertilizer use

Soil regeneration: Restoring degraded land by balancing soil nutrients through microbial activity



3️⃣ Transforming Agricultural Production with the BioValley Model

AGRIX is building the BioValley Model, an integrated system of agriculture, livestock, food processing, and distribution, to establish a sustainable agricultural economy.

Enhancing crop quality: Producing nutrient-rich crops with high polyphenol content

Reducing food loss: Extending shelf life through MBT55-enhanced composting

New revenue models: Providing investment opportunities in carbon credits and sustainable agriculture
