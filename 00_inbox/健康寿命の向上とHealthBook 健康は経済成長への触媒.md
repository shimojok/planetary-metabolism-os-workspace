マッキンゼーの報告によれば、健康を改善することは個人の生活の質を向上させるだけでなく、国家全体の生産性を大幅に引き上げる可能性を秘めています。しかし、医療技術の進歩により平均寿命は延びた一方で、生活習慣病や慢性的な健康被害の増加が深刻化しており、各国の医療費負担は財政を圧迫しています。特に、気候変動による社会的課題が増加する現代においては、イノベーションによる包括的な解決策が求められています。

健康寿命を延ばすことは、すべての人の生産性向上の鍵です。健康寿命の延伸は、高齢化社会における経済的・社会的課題の解決に直結します。健康な労働人口を増やし、医療費の削減を図ることは、長期的に経済成長を支える基盤となります。

日本での試算によると、2040年までに健康負担を30%削減することが可能であり、GDPの効果は、5,380億ドルとされています。そのためには、予防医療や個別化された健康管理が不可欠です。これは単なる医療技術の進化ではなく、ヘルスケア全体を効率化し、個人の健康を総合的にサポートするプラットフォームの必要性を示しています。

#### Health is a catalyst for economic growth and the foundation for productivity improvement.

According to a McKinsey report, improving health not only enhances individuals' quality of life but also significantly boosts national productivity. However, while advancements in medical technology have extended life expectancy, the increasing prevalence of lifestyle-related diseases and chronic health issues has become a serious concern, exacerbating the financial strain on healthcare systems worldwide. In today’s society, burdened by challenges such as climate change, comprehensive solutions driven by innovation are more critical than ever.

Extending healthy life expectancy is key to improving productivity for all. Extending healthy life expectancy directly addresses the economic and social challenges posed by aging populations. Increasing the proportion of healthy workers and reducing healthcare costs form the foundation for sustainable economic growth. 

According to estimates in Japan, it is possible to reduce the health burden by 30% by 2040, with an estimated GDP impact of $538 billion. Achieving this requires a focus on preventive medicine and personalized health management. This necessity points to the need for a comprehensive platform that not only advances medical technology but also streamlines healthcare and holistically supports individual health.

#### The significance of Amazon acquiring the HealthBook business

Amazon has already established a world-leading ecosystem through its advanced use of data, logistics, AI, and cloud services. By leveraging this technological foundation, Amazon can integrate the offerings of HealthBook—such as life insurance, nutritional diagnostics, gut microbiome testing, blood tests, metabolic analysis, and personalized nutrition programs and science-based foods—to deliver groundbreaking innovation in the healthcare sector.

Moreover, through Amazon's extensive customer network, these services can be made accessible to all, helping bridge health disparities and contributing to global productivity improvement. This initiative would address challenges posed by climate change and aging societies comprehensively, laying the groundwork for a sustainable future. Amazon’s acquisition of HealthBook is not just a growth strategy for the company; it is a strategic choice aimed at improving societal welfare and achieving economic sustainability on a global scale.
