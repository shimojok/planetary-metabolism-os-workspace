# [Selection of field projects for an Agroecological Transition in West Africa : From Innovation to Scale-up](https://www.araa.org/en/opportunities/selection-field-projects-agroecological-transition-west-africa-innovation-scale)

-   [Description](https://www.araa.org/en/opportunities/selection-field-projects-agroecological-transition-west-africa-innovation-scale#tab-7878-1)
-   [Dossier à télécharger](https://www.araa.org/en/opportunities/selection-field-projects-agroecological-transition-west-africa-innovation-scale#tab-7878-2)

The Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS), through the Lomé-based Regional Agency for Agriculture and Food (RAAF) and with the financial support of the Agence Française de Développement (AFD) and the European Union (EU) as a contribution to the DéSIRA+ Project in West Africa, is making available a total envelope of twelve million (12,000,000) Euros to finance field projects for an Agroecological Transition in West Africa: From Innovation to Scale-Up.

Funding will be granted to high-quality projects whose results will help to increase the incomes and resilience of producers in the agro-sylvo-pastoral and fisheries sectors to climate change by mobilising agroecological processes to intensify production.

Information on eligible organisations, instructions for formulating proposals and the administrative documentation to be provided are set out in the Call for Proposals Dossier (CfP) available on the following websites:

-   ECOWAS website : [www.ecowas.int](http://www.ecowas.int/) ;
-   CILSS website : [www.cilss.bf](http://www.cilss.bf/) ;
-   CORAF website : [https://www.coraf.org/](https://www.coraf.org/);
-   RAAF website : [https://araa.org/en/appels-projets](https://araa.org/en/appels-projets) ;
-   AFD DgMarket : [afd.dgmarket.com](http://www.afd.dgmarket.com/).
