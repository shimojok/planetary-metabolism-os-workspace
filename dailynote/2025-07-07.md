We don't have experience using MBT55 on coffee trees, but we have extensive experience cultivating apples, peaches, cherries, melons, and plums.
In addition to improving quality and yield, MBT55 significantly extends the lifespan of trees by optimizing the microbiome of their bark.
MBT55 also completely breaks down lignin and cellulose in coffee grounds within 24 hours, generating functional materials.
