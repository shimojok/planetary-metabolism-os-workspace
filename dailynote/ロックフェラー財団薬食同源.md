The concept of "Yakushoku Dogen" (薬食同源) posits that food and medicine share the same origin. It's the idea that by properly managing one's diet, health can be maintained and illness prevented. This philosophy originated in traditional Chinese medicine and has been adopted into Japanese food culture.
However, simply adhering to this principle is often insufficient for sustaining optimal health. We are going a step further, moving beyond just nutrient intake to metabolic improvement. We're taking on the challenge of promoting health through "MBT Food & Herbal Probiotics," which are already being utilized in the prevention of colonic diseases.
